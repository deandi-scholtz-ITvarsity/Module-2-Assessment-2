DEADPOOL

Wade Wilson, a former Special Forces operative turned mercenary, undergoes an experimental procedure hoping to cure his cancer. Instead, he develops accelerated healing abilities but becomes horribly disfigured. Reinventing himself as Deadpool, he embarks on a violent and comedic mission to hunt down the man responsible for his transformation. Filled with fourth-wall breaks, outrageous humour, and action, the film follows Deadpool's chaotic quest for revenge and redemption.
