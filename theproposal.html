THE PROPOSAL

Margaret Tate, a strict and demanding book editor, faces sudden deportation to Canada. In a desperate move, she forces her assistant, Andrew Paxton, into a fake marriage to keep her job. The pair travel to Alaska to convince his skeptical family the relationship is real, but their charade brings unexpected complications. As they spend more time together, genuine feelings begin to surface, blurring the line between pretense and reality.
