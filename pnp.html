PICK N PAY

Pick n Pay is one of South Africa’s most trusted supermarket chains, offering quality groceries and household essentials at competitive prices. With a strong commitment to freshness, customers can always expect top-quality produce, bakery items, meat, and dairy products. Their wide selection ensures that families can find everything they need under one roof.

TCustomer value lies at the heart of Pick n Pay’s philosophy, and this shines through in their popular Smart Shopper loyalty programme. With personalised discounts, cashback rewards, and special deals, Smart Shopper helps customers save more with every visit. Pick n Pay continues to invest in making the shopping experience rewarding and convenient for all.

As a brand known for its community involvement, Pick n Pay actively supports local suppliers and charitable initiatives across South Africa. Their focus on giving back, combined with reliable service and clean, organised stores, makes Pick n Pay a household favourite nationwide.
