HOW TO TRAIN YOUR DRAGON

In the Viking village of Berk, young Hiccup dreams of proving himself as a dragon-slaying warrior. Everything changes when he injures a rare Night Fury dragon and chooses to befriend it instead of killing it. As their bond grows, Hiccup discovers that dragons are misunderstood creatures, leading him to challenge his tribe's long-held beliefs. When a deadly threat emerges, Hiccup and his dragon, Toothless, must work together to save both the dragons and the people of Berk.
