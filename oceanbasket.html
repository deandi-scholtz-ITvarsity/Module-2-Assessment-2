OCEAN BASKET

Ocean Basket is South Africa's favourite seafood restaurant, offering fresh, flavourful dishes inspired by Mediterranean cuisine. From grilled fish to sushi, platters, prawns, and seafood pasta, the restaurant provides something for everyone who appreciates the taste of the ocean. Their commitment to freshness and generous portions sets them apart.

The warm, relaxed atmosphere of Ocean Basket makes it ideal for casual dining, celebrations, and family meals. With friendly service and meals designed for sharing, customers can enjoy a laid-back yet satisfying dining experience. It's a place where flavour, comfort, and hospitality come together.

Ocean Basket also focuses heavily on sustainability and responsible sourcing, ensuring customers can enjoy seafood with peace of mind. Their dedication to quality and ethical practices continues to strengthen their reputation as a trusted and loved seafood brand across South Africa and beyond.
